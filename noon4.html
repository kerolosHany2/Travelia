Travelers' Choice Awards Best of the Best Restaurants

If food is the way to your heart, our Travelers’ Choice Awards Best of the Best lists are the place to start. These restaurants have left an impression on travelers for their incredible flavors. From fancy spots to hidden gems, they will leave you craving for more.



Fine dining

When you’re looking for the crème-de-la-crème, these are the places worth getting on the waitlist for. Think: white tablecloths, best-in-class chefs, and unforgettable bites.

Date night

Whether it’s the stunning backdrop or the cozy vibe, something about these restaurants really sets the mood for a night of wining and dining.

Casual dining

These restaurants might be casual, but there’s nothing casual about how much travelers love them. Because really good food and really good vibes are a winning combo.

Hidden games

You have to get a bit off the beaten path to find these spots, but they certainly earn their winner status in both taste and local charm.

Vegan & Vegetarian

The menus at these winning spots are plant-based, vegan, or vegetarian friendly, but that’s not even the headline—the food would stand out in any category (it’s that good).

Quick bites

Sometimes the best meals are the ones you least expect. That’s where these winners come in: no frills, all flavor, and best enjoyed on the go.

Outdoor

Dine al fresco at these restaurants that win not just for their patios and terraces, but for so much more: the menu, the ambience, the service (we could go on).

Pet-Friendly

Good news for dogs (and their humans) everywhere: The whole family is welcome at these traveler-loved restaurants.

Sustainable

For eco-conscious diners, these winners stand out for their commitment to sustainability as measured by a combination of inputs from our data partners at BeCause and internal Tripadvisor data points, including restaurants where Tripadvisor users were most likely to be found discussing sustainability in positive reviews.
